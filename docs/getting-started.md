# Getting Started

This page is a how-to guide for generating numbers in the app.

## Using the App

### Step 1: Visit the Website

Open your web browser and go to:

**[Visit the app](https://mheydon73-lottery-generator.azurewebsites.net/)**

### Step 2: Choose Your Game

You'll see a home page displaying all the available lottery games:

- **Lotto** – Choose 6 numbers from 1 to 59
- **EuroMillions** – Choose 5 main numbers and 2 Lucky Stars
- **Thunderball** – Choose 5 main numbers and 1 Thunderball
- **Set For Life** – Choose 5 main numbers and 1 Life Ball
- **Lotto HotPicks** – Pick 1 to 5 numbers from Lotto draws
- **EuroMillions HotPicks** – Pick 1 to 5 numbers from EuroMillions draws

Click on the game you want to play.

### Step 3: View Your Generated Numbers

The app will instantly display:

- **Suggested Lines** – Your main number suggestions
- **Other Suggestions** – Additional lines to consider
- **Latest Draw Date** – When the most recent draw took place

That's it! Your numbers are ready to use.

### Step 4: Try Another Game

Want numbers for a different game? Click the **"Other Games"** menu button at the top of the page to switch games, or click **"← All Games"** to return to the home page.

## Understanding the Results

### Main Suggested Lines

These are the primary number suggestions based on the app's analysis of historical draws. The algorithm looks at patterns and frequencies to create these lines.

### Other Suggestions

These are alternative lines generated using slightly different approaches. They give you more options to choose from.

### Latest Draw Date

The app shows you when the most recent draw data was collected. This helps you know the data is current.

## Tips for Using the App

💡 **Generate fresh numbers regularly** – The app updates with new draw data automatically, so you can come back anytime for fresh suggestions.

💡 **Mix and match** – Feel free to combine numbers from different lines if you prefer.

💡 **Compare games** – Try generating numbers for different games to see which ones appeal to you.

💡 **Do not take it too seriously** – This is just for fun. The lottery is random, and no algorithm can predict results.

## Important Reminders

⚠️ **This is entertainment, not a prediction tool.** The National Lottery draws are completely random. Past results don't influence future outcomes.

⚠️ **The numbers generated are no more likely to win than random numbers.** They're created using a playful algorithm, not a scientific prediction method.

⚠️ **Always gamble responsibly.** Only spend what you can afford to lose, and remember that lottery games are meant to be fun.
